About the Queens of Clean

So, what's to love about us?

We are three women that are longtime residents in Dutchess County. You will be getting locally working women instead of big corporate cleaning businesses. We all come from families with children so we know how messy the house can get. That's why we charge one flat rate instead of charging by the hour.

What makes you better than other cleaning companies?

We are a small business comprised of only three women. Why is this important? We love being able to meet one on one with our clients to ensure they are receiving the best service possible. We use only the best cleaning products leaving your space place spic and span.

Where do we work?

As we mentioned before, we are a locally run business. That being said, we currently service houses in the Dutchess County area. We are looking to expand our services in the near future. This smaller area range allows us to arrive faster if cleaning services are needed last minute.

What else do we do?

On top of making your space spotless, we also offer organizational services. Whether you need cleaning or organizing done, we can provide you with a free estimate.

Our Mission

At the Queens of Clean, our goal is to allow you to relax and take care of more pressing issues while we do the cleaning. We know cleaning can take a while and we want to provide you with enough time for loved ones, hobbies, and time for yourself.

Location

We currently service houses in the Dutchess County area. We are looking to expand our services in the near future.
