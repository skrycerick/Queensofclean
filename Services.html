Services

We offer a wide variety of services to suit all needs. We specialize in move-in cleanings, move-out cleanings, and even renovation clean up. We do not charge an hourly rate in order to put forth our best offer because we know some projects take longer than others.

What we do:

We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly cleanings at a flat rate.


  1. Residential Cleanings

    Families:
    We offer residential cleanings to families in single-family and multi-family homes and apartments. Our first visit will be a detailed clean of the house which will bring your home to its best. Following, based on your service, we will return and focus on high traffic areas and then move to other areas to keep your home looking beautiful.

    Landlords:
    We can help you provide a nice clean home for your new residents. We offer move in and move out services for landlords that will allow you to ensure your residents are happy that they chose your rental.


  2. Commercial Cleanings

    Office Owners:
    Our service will ensure your employees are in a comfortable working environment. Whether it is a large office of cubicles or a small call center, we will make it our priority that your employees feel important and cared for.

    Retail Owners:
    Customers love a clean store and with our help you can provide that. We can ensure your customers will feel appreciated and they will acknowledge your efforts to providing the best customer experience.


  3. Post-Renovation:

    Whether you recently remodeled your kitchen, bathroom, or office space, we will be there to help your hard work shine after the remodel is completed.
